What is sentiment analysis?

According to the Oxford Dictionary, sentiment analysis is the process of computationally identifying and categorizing opinions expressed in a piece of text, especially in order to determine whether the writer's attitude towards a particular topic, product, etc. is positive, negative, or neutral. As such, CryptoCurrently wants to utilize these sentiments in order to create trading indicators and provide traders with more insights to make informed decisions. Additionally, CryptoCurrently also uses natural language processing techniques such as DTM, Ngram, TF IDF to provide traders with more data to consider, as well as the use of bar graphs and Wordcloud in order to create powerful data visualizations.
